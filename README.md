FoodPicker 是一款简洁实用的美食选择助手，专为解决日常吃饭纠结、外卖选择困难打造。支持随机推荐美食、自定义饮食偏好、快速筛选菜品，轻松搞定每日用餐选择，让选餐更高效、更省心。

FoodPicker is a simple and practical food decision helper designed to solve daily meal indecision and takeout choice difficulties. It supports random food recommendations, customizable dietary preferences, and quick dish filtering, making every meal choice efficient and hassle-free.
